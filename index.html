ABOUT RGIPT

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT), Jais, is an autonomous institution established by the Government of India. The Institute has been accorded the status of “An Institution of National Importance” along the lines of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) under an Act of Parliament (“Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Act 2007”). RGIPT is co-promoted as an energy domain specific institute by six leading Oil Public Sector Units (ONGC, IOCL, OIL, GAIL, BPCL and HPCL) in association with the Oil Industry Development Board (OIDB). The Institute is associated with leading international universities and institutions specializing in the domain of Petroleum & Energy Technology.

ABOUT WINTER SCHOOL

Winter School is a technological instruction programme offered by the Science & Technical Committee of the RGIPT in which pupils study topics that are outside the scope of a typical classroom. In this continuously changing environment, an ideal engineer's objective must be to maintain his skill set up to date. However, the majority of what we studying academic institutions is knowledge, but the preponderance of what we need in our employment is skills. That's what we're trying to achieve with Winter School 2022: Seek Competence, make students competent for the fast-moving world and equip them with pertinent skills to succeed in the professional front.

EVENTS AND COMPETITIONS

  • Scout the Untold Tech

    Challenging the brains of the participants with the tricky questions of the tech field, this event will be a technical treasure hunt round where participants will be provided with one question and they have to use their logical approach and technical knowledge to make route through the answers, as that will be the hint of next question.

  • BGMI GIANT GLIDERS

    The world of gaming is alluring and enchanting. Amidse technical education and tough competition, we introduce a thrilling gaming break in the form of "Giant Gliders". The participants will be flaunting their advancing skills in BGMI. The live commentary throughout the gaming is going to turn this event more exciting.

  • UXaspiring Battle

    In the modern era of technology where people are very fond of websites and mobile application, we need to maintain a great User Interface to give good Experience, hence along with technical skills, students also needs to have a great Designing taste, UXaspiring Battle is such a UI/UX challenge to uplift the student’s designing skills.

  • Notion Unveiling

    Along with Academics, it is very important for the students to get the practical aspects of the concepts and methodology. Projects are the best to ensure this, Notion Unveiling is a contest in which student have to present their Project, Independent of the subject or field.

  • Hackathon

    Are you the type of person that won't stop until they've found a solution? Do you enjoy taking on new challenges? Bringing you Hackathons that will engage both the left and right sides of your brain. A wonderful approach to improve your problem-solving and creative abilities. If you believe you can easily integrate your concept into an executable solution, participate in this Hackathon. To put your programming and problem-solving talents to the test, complete this coding challenge.

  • MUN

    The Model United Nations is an attempt to encourage healthy debate and discussion about many problems and hurdles from a youthful perspective, as well as sensible and practical solutions. Our dedicated MUN team will propose the most engaging motions in order to facilitate the highest possible discussion quality.

Science and Technology (S & T) Council is the student technical activities body of the Institute. As an umbrella organization, it coordinates with Tinkering Lab, Kode Club, E-Cell and organizes Annual Technical Festivals Urjotsav. The main role of the council is to foster the technical, entrepreneurial, and management skills in the students by providing a platform to convert ideas to prototypes and subsequently from prototypes to products. The council organizes various expert lectures, workshops, projects as well as competitions.
